“We fear we don`t have a home to come back ”

16 years old Monica Kharel is one of the refugees in Tundikhel, one of the main parks in Nepal, where hundreds sleep in fear. The Earthquake ocurred while she was in the upper floor of a mall in the center of Katmandu. She remembers "I screamed as if the reaper followed me until I found reached the floor"

Rains, lack of communications and the aftershocks have made more complicated the search of survivors, The Red Cross is giving help -first aids, rescue teams and assistance-, but there are thoudands of them that need help
